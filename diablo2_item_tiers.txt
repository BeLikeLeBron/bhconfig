* v0.1, 5/11/19: first release

##Rationale
Probably most helpful to think of items in tiers. When muling, I would throw out the lowest tier first. Assume average roll for listed position, otherwise knock it up or down a tier. These tiers aren’t a reflection of how good I think the item is, but some combination of usefulness, rarity, and trophy status. So even though shako is in tier 3, that doesn’t mean it’s not the BIS helm for a bunch of different builds.

Another way to say it is that these are ranked by how painful it would be for me to leave the item on the ground.
